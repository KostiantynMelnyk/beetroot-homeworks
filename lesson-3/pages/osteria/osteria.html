#1 RESTARAUNT IN THE WORLD

HOME

Italian cuisine is recognized all over the world. Its history is extensive, and its dishes are self-sufficient. Any attempts to make any changes to it, as a rule, will be met with hostility, and everyone who tries will be scolded by noisy Italians who cherish their history and its gastronomic traditions. An even more impossible task is to try to change the culinary traditions, being in the provinces, and not in the capital, where the local population mixes with numerous tourists open to gastronomic experiments. As the story of ambitious chef Massimo Bottura and his Osteria Francescana, the best restaurant in the world, shows, everything is possible if you believe in what you do and do what you believe in, and there is a person who believes in you.

June 19 in Spanish Bilbao were named the 50 best restaurants in the world. The ceremony was organized by the British publication Restaurant, which has been compiling The World's 50 Best Restaurants annually since 2002. This year, the list was topped by the Italian restaurant Osteria Francescana by renowned chef Massimo Bottura. For the latter, victorious laurels are not new. The restaurant already topped this list in 2016, and in 2017 lost first place to New York's Eleven Madison Park, only to return with a victory this year. However, the path to this peak was long and thorny.
